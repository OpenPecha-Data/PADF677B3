trayodaśo'dhikāraḥpratipattivibhāge ṣaṭ ślokāḥ|dvedhā nairātmyamājñāya dhīmān pudgaladharmayoḥ|dvayamithyātvasamyaktvaṃ
vivarjyeta trayeṇa hi

arthajñaḥ sarvadharmāṇāṃ vetti kolasamānatāṃ|śrutatuṣṭiprahāṇāya dharmajñastena kathyate

pārthagjanena jñānena pratividhya dvayaṃ tathā|tajjñānapariniṣpattāvanudharmaṃ prapadyate

tato jñānaṃ sa labhate lokottaramanuttaraṃ|ādibhūmau samaṃ sarvairbodhisattvaistadātmabhiḥ

kṛtvā darśanajñeyānāṃ[heyānāṃ] kleśānāṃ sarvasaṃkṣayam|jñeyāvaraṇajñānāya[hānāya] bhavanāyāṃ
prayujyate

vyavasthānāvikalpena jñānena sahacāriṇā|anudharmaṃ caratyevaṃ pariśiṣṭāsu bhūmiṣu

sulābho 'tha svadhiṣṭhānaḥ subhūmiḥ susahāyakaḥ|suyogo guṇavān deśo yatra dhīmān prapadyate

bahuśruto dṛṣṭasatyo vāgmī samanukampakaḥ|akhinno bodhisattvaśca jñeyaḥ satpurūṣo mahān

svālambanā susaṃ bhārā [susaṃstabdhā] subhāvanaiva [supāyācaiva ?] deśitā|suniryāṇaprayogā ca
ātmasamyakpradhānatā

rateḥ kṣaṇopapatteśca ārogyasyāpi kāraṇaṃ|samādhervicayasyāpi pūrve hi kṛtapuṇyatā

dharmadhātuvinirmukto yasmāddharmo na vidyate|tasmādrāgādayasteṣāṃ buddhairniḥsaraṇaṃ matāḥ

dharmadhātuvinirmukto yasmāddharmo na vidyate|tasmātsaṃkleśanirdeśe sa saṃvid[saṃdhira]dhīmatāṃ
mataḥ

yatastāneva rāgādīnyoniśaḥ pratipadyate|tato vimucyate tebhyastenaiṣāṃ niḥsṛtistataḥ

na khalu jinasutānāṃ bādhakaṃ duḥkhamugraṃnarakabhavanavāsaiḥ satvahetoḥ kathaṃcit|śamabhavaguṇadoṣapreritā
hīnayānevividhaśubhavikalpā bādhakā dhīmatāṃ tu

na khalu narakavāso dhīmatāṃ sarvakālaṃvimalavipulabodherantarāyaṃ karoti|svahitaparamaśītastvanyayāne
vikalpaḥparamasukhavihāre 'pyantarāyaṃ karoti

dharmābhāvopalabdhiśca niḥsaṃkleśaviśuddhitā|māyādisadṛśī jñeyā ākāśasadṛśī tathā

yathaiva citre vidhivadvicitrite natonnataṃ nāsti ca dṛśyate'tha ca|abhūtakalpe 'pi tathaiva
sarvathā dvayaṃ sadā nāsti ca dṛśyate 'the ca

yathaiva toye luti[ṭi]te prasādite na jāyate sā punaracchatānyataḥ|malāpakarṣastu sa tatra kevalaḥ
svacittaśuddhau vidhireṣa eva hi

mataṃ ca cittaṃ prakṛtiprabhāsvaraṃ sadā tadāgantukadoṣadūṣitaṃ|na dharmatācittamṛte 'nyacetasaḥ
prabhāsvaratvaṃ prakṛtau vidhīyate

bodhisattvasya sattveṣu prema majjagataṃ mahat|yathaikaputrake tasmātsadā hitakaraṃ matam

satveṣu hitakāritvānnaityāpattiṃ sa rāgajāṃ|dveṣo virudyate tvasya sarvasatveṣu satpathā[sarvathā]

yathā kapotī svasutātivatsalā svabhāvakāṃstānupaguhya tiṣṭhati|tathāvidhāyaṃ pratigho virudhyate
suteṣu tadvatsakṛpe 'pi dehiṣu

maitrī yataḥ pratighacittamato viruddhaṃśāntiryato vyasanacittamato viruddhaṃ|artho yato nikṛticittamato
viruddhaṃlhādo yataḥ pratibhayaṃ na[ca] tato viruddhaṃ

yathāturaḥ subhaiṣajye saṃsāre pratipadyate|āture ca yathā vaidyaḥ satveṣu pratipadyate

aniṣpanne yathā ceṭe svātmani pratipadyate|vaṇigyathā punaḥ puṇye kāmeṣu pratipadyate

yathaiva rajako vastre karmaṇe pratipadyate|pitā yathā sute bāle satvāheṭhe prapadyate

agnyarthī vādharāraṇyāṃ sātatye pratipadyate|vaiśvāsiko vāniṣpanne adhicitte prapadyate

māyākāra iva jñeye prajñayā pratipadyate|pratipattiryathā yasmin bodhisattvasya sā matā

iti satatamudārayuktavīryo dvayaparipācanaśodhane suyuktaḥ|paramavimalanirvikalpabuddhyā vrajati
sa siddhimanuttamāṃ krameṇa

|| mahāyānasūtrālaṃkāre pratipattyadhikārastrayodaśaḥ||
