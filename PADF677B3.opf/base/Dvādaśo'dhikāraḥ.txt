vācā padaiḥ suyuktairanudeśavibhāgasaṃśayacchedaiḥ|bahulīkārānugatā hyuddhaṭitavipañcitajñeṣu

śuddhā trimaṇḍalena hiteyaṃ deśanā hi buddhānāṃ|doṣairvivarjitā punaraṣṭabhireṣaiva vijñeyā

parairasādhāraṇayogakevalaṃ tridhātukakleśavihānipūrakam|svabhāvaśuddhaṃ malaśuddhitaśca taccaturguṇabrahmavicaryamiṣyate
