ekonaviṃśatyadhikāraḥāścarya vibhāge trayaḥ ślokāḥ |svadehasya parityāgaḥ saṃpatteścaiva saṃvṛttau|durbaleṣu
kṣamā kāye jīvite nirapekṣiṇaḥ

vīryārambho hyanāsvādo dhāneṣu sukha eva ca|niṣkalpanā na prajñāyāmāścaryaṃ dhīmatāṃ ga[ma]taṃ

tathāgatakule janmalābho vyākaraṇasya ca|abhiṣekasya ca prāptirbodheścāścaryamiṣyate

vairāgyaṃ karuṇāṃ caitya bhāvanāṃ paramāmapi|tathaiva samacittatvaṃ nāścaryaṃ tāsu yuktatā

na tathātmani dāreṣu sutamitreṣu bandhuṣu|satvānāṃ pragataḥ sneho yathā satveṣu dhīmatāṃ

arthiṣvapakṣapātaśca śīlasyākhaṇḍanā dhruvaṃ|kṣāntiḥ sarvatra satvārthaṃ[sarvārthaṃ]vīryārambho
mahānapi

dhyānaṃ ca kuśalaṃ nityaṃ prajñā caivāvikalpikā|vijñeyā bodhisatvānāṃ tāsveva samacittatā

sthāpanā bhājanatve ca śīleṣveva ca ropaṇaṃ|marṣaṇā cāpakārasya arthe vyāpāragāmitā

āvarjanā śāsane 'smiṃśchedanā saṃśayasya ca|satveṣu upakāritvaṃ dhīmatāmetadiṣyate

samāśayena satvānāṃ dhārayanti sadaiva ye|janayantyāryabhūmau ca kuśalairvardhayanti ca

duṣkṛtātparirakṣanti śrutaṃ vyutpādayanti ca|pañcabhiḥ karmabhiḥ satvamātṛkalpā jinātmajāḥ

śraddhāyāḥ sarvasatveṣu sarvadā cāvaropaṇāt|adhiśīlādiśikṣāyāṃ vimuktau ca niyojanāt

buddhādhyeṣaṇataścaiṣāmāvṛteśca vivarjanāt|pañcabhiḥ karmabhiḥ satvapitṛkalpā jinātmajāḥ

anarhadeśanāṃ ye ca satvānāṃ gūhayanti hi|śikṣāvipattiṃ nindanti śaṃsantyeva ca saṃpadam

avavādaṃ ca yacchanti mārānāvedayanti hi|pañcabhiḥ karmabhiḥ sattvabandhukalpā jinātmajāḥ

saṃkleśe vyavadāne ca svayamaśrāntabuddhayaḥ|yacchanti laukikīṃ kṛtsnāṃ saṃpadaṃ cātilaukikīm

sukhe hite cābhinnā[akheditvādabhinnā] ye sadā sukhahitaiṣiṇaḥ|pañcabhiḥ karmabhiḥ satvamitrakalpā
jinātmajāḥ

sarvadodyamavanto ye satvānāṃ paripācane|samyagniryāṇavaktāraḥ kṣamā vipratipattiṣu

dvayasaṃpattidātārastadupāye ca kovidāḥ|pañcabhiḥ karmabhiḥ satvadāsakalpā jinātmajāḥ

anutpattikadharmeṣu kṣāntiṃ prāptāśca ye matāḥ|sarvayā[no]padeṣṭāraḥ sidvayogāniyojakāḥ

sumukhāḥ pratikāre ca vipāke cānapekṣiṇaḥ|pañcabhiḥ karmabhiḥ satvācāryakalpā jinātmajāḥ

satvakṛtyārthamudyuktāḥ saṃbhārānpūrayanti ye|saṃbhṛtānmocayantyāśu vipakṣaṃ hāpayanti ca

lokasaṃpattibhiścitrairalokairyojayanti ca|pañcabhiḥ karmabhiḥ satvopādhyāyakalpā jinātmajāḥ

asaktyā caiva bhogeṣu śīlasya ca na khaṇḍanaiḥ|kṛtajñatānuyogācca pratipattau ca yogataḥ

ṣaṭsu pāramitāsveva vartamānā hi dehinaḥ|bhavanti bodhisatvānāṃ tathā pratyupakāriṇaḥ

vṛddhiṃ hāniṃ ca kāṅkṣanti satvānāṃ ca prapācanaṃ|viśeṣagamanaṃ bhūmau bodhiṃ cānuttarāṃ sadā

trāsahānau samutpāde saṃśayacchedane 'pi ca|pratipattyavavāde ca sadābandhyā jinātmajāḥ

dānaṃ niṣpratikāṅkṣasya niḥspṛhasya punarbhave|śīlaṃ kṣāntiśca sarvatra vīryaṃ sarvaśubhodaye

vinā[ā?]rūpyaṃ tathā dhyānaṃ prajñā copāyasaṃhitā|samyakprayogo dhīrāṇāṃ ṣaṭsu pāramitāsuhi

bhogasaktiḥ sacchidratvaṃ mānaścaiva sukhallikā|āsvādanaṃ vikalpaśca dhīrāṇāṃ hānihetavaḥ

sthitānāṃ bodhisatvānāṃ pratipakṣeṣu teṣu ca|jñeyā viśeṣabhāgīyā dharmā etadviparyayāt

pravā[tā]raṇāpi kuhanā saumukhyasya ca darśanā|lobhatvena tatha vṛttiḥ śāntavākkāyatā tathā

suvākkaraṇasaṃpacca pratipattivivarjitā|ete hi bodhisatvānāmabhūtatvāya deśitāḥ|viparyayātprayuktānāṃ
tadbhūtatvāya deśitāḥ

te dānādyupaṃsahāraiḥ satvānāṃ vinayanti hi|ṣaṭprakāraṃ vipakṣaṃ hi dhīmantaḥ sarvabhūmiṣu

dhīmadvyākaraṇaṃ dvedhā kālapudgalabhedataḥ|bodhau vyākaraṇe caiva mahāccānyadudāhṛtaṃ

notpattikṣāntilābhena mānābhogavihānitaḥ|ekībhāvagamatvācca sarvabuddhajinātmajaiḥ

kṣetreṇaṃ nāmnā kālena kalpanāmnā ca tatpunaḥ|parivārānuvṛtyā ca saddharmasya tadiṣyate

saṃpatyutpattinaiyamyapāto 'khede ca dhīmatāṃ|bhāvanāyāśca sātatye samādhānācyutāvapi|kṛtyasiddhāvanābhoge
kṣāntilābhe ca sarvathā

pūjā śikṣāsamādānaṃ karuṇā śubhabhāvanā|apramādastathāraṇye śrutārthātṛptireva ca|sarvabhūmiṣu
dhīrāṇāmavaśyakaraṇīyatā

kāmeṣvādīnavajñānaṃ skhaliteṣu nirīkṣaṇā|duḥkhādhivāsanā caiva kuśalasya ca bhāvanā

anāsvādaḥ sukhe caiva nimittānāmakalpanā|sātatyakaraṇīyaṃ hi dhīmatāṃ sarvabhūmiṣu

dharmadānaṃ śīlaśuddhirnotpattikṣāntireva ca|vīryārambho mahāyāne antyā sakaruṇā sthitiḥ|prajñā
pāramitānāṃ ca pradhānaṃ dhīmatāṃ matam

vidyāsthānavyavasthānaṃ sūtrādyākārabhedataḥ|jñeyaṃ dharmavyavasthānaṃ dhīmatāṃ sarvabhūmiṣu

punaḥ satvavyavasthānaṃ saptadhā tathatāśrayāt|caturdhā ca tridhā caiva yuktiyānavyavasthitiḥ

yoniśaśca manaskāraḥ samyagdṛṣṭiḥ phalānvitā|pramāṇairvicayo 'cintyaṃ jñeyaṃ yukticatuṣṭayam

āśayāddeśanāccaiva prayogātsaṃbhṛterapi|samudāgamabhedācca trividhaṃ yānamiṣyate

āgantukatvaparyeṣā anyonyaṃ nāmavastunoḥ|prajñapterdvividhasyātra tanmātratvasya vaiṣaṇā

sarvasyānupalambhācca bhūtajñānaṃ caturvidhaṃ|sarvārthasiddhyai dhīrāṇāṃ sarvabhūmiṣu jāyate

pratiṣṭhābhogabījaṃ hi nimittaṃ bandhanasya hi|sāśrayāścittacaittāstu badhyante 'tra sabījakāḥ

purataḥ sthāpitaṃ yacca nimittaṃ yatsthitaṃ svayaṃ|sarvaṃ vibhāvayandhīmān labhate bodhimuttamām

tathatālambanaṃ jñānaṃ dvayagrāhavivarjitaṃ|dauṣṭhulyakāyapratyakṣaṃ tatkṣaye dhīmatāṃ matam

tathatālambanaṃ jñānamanānākārabhāvitaṃ|sadasattārthe pratyakṣaṃ vikalpavibhu cocyate

tattvaṃ saṃcchādya bālānāmatattvaṃ khyāti sarvataḥ|tattvaṃ tu bodhisatvānāṃ sarvataḥ khyātyapāsya
tat

akhyānakhyānatā jñeyā asadarthasadarthayoḥ|āśrayasya parāvṛttirmokṣo 'sau kāmacārataḥ

anyonyaṃ tulyajātīyaḥ khyātyarthaḥ sarvato mahān|antarāyakarastasmātparijñāyainamutsṛjet

paripācyaṃ viśodhyaṃ ca prāpyaṃ yogyaṃ ca pācane|samyaktvadeśanāvastu aprameyaṃ hi dhīmatām

bodhisatva[citta]sya cotpādo notpādakṣāntireva ca|cakṣuśca nirmalaṃ hīnamāśravakṣaya eva ca

saddharmasya sthitirdīrghā vyutpatticchittibhogatā|deśanāyāḥ phalaṃ jñeyaṃ tatprayuktasya dhīmataḥ

ālambanamahatvaṃ ca pratipatterdvayostathā|jñānasya vīryārambhasya upāye kauśalasya ca

udāgamamahatvaṃ ca mahatvaṃ buddhakarmaṇaḥ|etanmahatvayogāddhi mahāyānaṃ nirucyate

gotraṃ dharmādhimuktiśca cittasyotpādanā tathā|dānādipratipattiśca nyāyā[mā]vakrāntireva ca

satvānāṃ paripākaśca kṣetrasya ca viśodhanā|apratiṣṭhitanirvāṇaṃ bodhiḥ śreṣṭhā ca darśanāt[darśanā]

ādhimokṣika ekaśca śuddhādhyāśayiko 'paraḥ|nimitte cānimitte ca cāryapyanabhisaṃskṛte|bodhisatvā
hi vijñeyāḥ pañcaite sarvabhūmiṣu

kāmeṣvasaktasstriviśuddhakarmā krodhābhibhūmyaṃ guṇatatparaśca|dharme 'calastatvagabhīradṛṣṭirbodhau
spṛhāvān khalu bodhisattvaḥ

anugraheccho 'nupaghātadṛṣṭiḥ paropaghāteṣvadhivāsakaśca|dhīro 'pramattaśca bahuśrutaśca parārthayuktaḥ
khalu bodhisattvaḥ

ādīnavajñaḥ svaparigraheṣu bhogeṣvasakto hyanigūḍhavairaḥ|yogī nimitte kuśalo 'kudṛṣṭiradhyātmasaṃsthaḥ
khalu bodhisattvaḥ

dayānvito hrīguṇasaṃniviṣṭo duḥkhādhivāsātsvasukheṣvasaktaḥ|smṛtipradhānaḥ susamāhitātmā yānāvikāryaḥ
khalu bodhisattvaḥ

duḥkhāpaho duḥkhakaro na caiva duḥkhādhivāso na ca duḥkhabhītaḥ|duḥkhādvimukto na ca duḥkhakalpo
duḥkhābhyupetaḥ khalu bodhisattvaḥ

vaśī dharmanirandhakāro dharmapradhānaḥ khalu bodhisattvaḥ

bhogāpramatto niyamāpramatto rakṣāpramattaḥ kuśalāpramattaḥ|sukhāpramatto dharamāpramatto yānāpramatto
