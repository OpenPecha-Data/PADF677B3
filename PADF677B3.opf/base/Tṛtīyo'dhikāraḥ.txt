dvidhaupamyaṃ caturvidhā

dhātūnāmadhimukteśca pratipatteśca bhedataḥ|phalabhedopalabdheśca gotrāstitvaṃ nirūpyate

udagratve'tha sarvatve mahārthatve'kṣayāya ca|śubhasya tatrimittatvāt gotrāgratvaṃ vidhīyate

prakṛtyā paripuṣṭaṃ ca āśrayaścāśritaṃ ca tat|sadasaccaiva vijñeyaṃ guṇottāraṇatārthataḥ

kāruṇyamadhimuktiśca kṣāntiścādiprayogataḥ|samācāraḥ subhasyāpi gotreliṅgaṃ nirupyate

niyatāniyataṃ gotramahāryaṃ hāryameva ca|pratyairgotrabhedo 'yaṃ samāsena caturvidhaḥ

kleśābhyāsaḥ kumitratvaṃ vidhātaḥ paratantratā|gotrasyādīnavo jñeyaḥ samāsena caturvidhaḥ

cirādapāyagamanamāśumokṣaśca tatra ca|tanuduḥkhopasaṃvittiḥ sodvegā sattvapācanā

suvarṇagotravat jñeyamameyaśubhatāśrayaḥ|jñānanirmalatāyogaprabhāvāṇāṃ ca niśrayaḥ
