pañcadaśo'dhikāraḥuddānamaupāyasahitakarmavibhāge catvāraḥ ślokāḥ |adhimukterbahulatā dharmaparyeṣṭideśane|pratipattistathā
samyagavavādānuśāsanaṃ

yathā pratiṣṭhā vanadehiparvatapravāhinīnāṃ pṛthivī samantataḥ|tathaiva dānādiśubhasya sarvato
budheṣu karma trividhaṃ nirucyate

suduṣkaraiḥ karmabhirudyatātmānāṃ vicitrarūpairbahukalpanirgataiḥ|na kāyavāk cittamayasya karmaṇo
jinātmajānāṃ bhavatīha saṃnatiḥ

yathā viṣācchastramahāśanād[ne] ripornivārayedātmahitaḥ svamāśrayaṃ|nihinayānadvividhājjinātmajo
nivārayetkarma tathā trayātmakaṃ

na karmiṇaḥ karma na karmaṇaḥ kriyāṃ sadāvikalpaḥ samudīkṣate tridhā|tato 'sya tatkarma viśuddhipāragaṃ
bhavatyanantaṃ tadupāyasaṃgrahāt

|| mahāyānasūtrālaṃkāre upāyasahitakarmādhikāraḥ pañcadaśaḥ||
