saptadaśo'dhikāraḥbuddhapūjāvibhāge sapta ślokāḥ |saṃmukhaṃ vimukhaṃ pūjā buddhānāṃ cīvarādibhiḥ|gāḍhaprasannacittasya
saṃbhāradvayapūraye

abandhyabuddhajanmatve praṇidhānavataḥ sataḥ|trayasyānupalambhastu niṣpannā buddhapūjā

satvānāmaprameyānāṃ paripākāya cāparā|upadheścittataścānyā adhimukternidhānataḥ

anukampākṣamābhyāṃ ca samudācārato 'parā|vastvābhogāvabodhācca vimukteśca tathātvataḥ

āśrayādvastutaḥ pūjā nimittātpariṇāmanāt|hetuto jñānataḥ kṣetrānniśrayācca pradarśitā

hetutaḥ phalataścaiva ātmanā ca parairapi|lābhasatkārataścaiva pratipatterdvidhā ca sā

parīttā mahatī pūjā samānāmānikā ca sā|prayogādgatitaścaiva praṇidhānācca sā matā

buddheṣu pūjā paramā svacittāt dharmādhimuktyāśayato vibhutvāt|akalpanopāyaparigraheṇa sarvaikakāryatvaniveśataśca

āśrayādvastutaḥ sevā nimittātpariṇāmanāt|hetuto jñānataḥ kṣetrānniśrayācca pradarśitā

mitraṃ śrayeddāntaśamopaśāntaṃ guṇādhikaṃ sodyamamāgamāḍhyaṃ|prabuddhatatvaṃ vacasābhyupetaṃ
kṛpātmakaṃ khedavivarjitaṃ ca

satkāralābhaiḥ paricaryayā ca seveta mitraṃ pratipattitaśca|dharme tathājñāśaya eva dhīmān mitraṃ
pragacchetsamaye nataśca

satkāralābheṣu gataspṛho 'sau prapattaye taṃ pariṇāmayecca|yathānuśiṣṭapratipattitaśca saṃrādhayeccittamato
'sya dhīraḥ

yānatraye kauśalametya buddhyā svasyaiva yānasya yateta siddhau|iti mānatrayakauśalāt jñānaṃ|satvānameyānparipācanāya
kṣetrasya śuddhasya ca sādhanāya

dharmeṣu dāyādaguṇena yukto naivāmiṣeṇa pravesatsa mitram|hetoḥ phalāddharmamukhānuyānātseveta
mitraṃ bahitaśca dhīmān

śrutaśravāccetasi yogataśca samānanirmānamano 'nuyogāt|gatiprayogapraṇidhānataśca kalyāṇamitraṃ
hi bhajet dhīmān

sanmitrasevā paramā svacittād dharmādhimuktyāśayato vibhutvaiḥ|akalpanopāyaparigraheṇa sarvaikakāryatvaniveśataśca

brāhmā vipakṣahīnā jñānena gatāśca nirvikalpena|trividhālambanavṛttāḥ satvānāṃ pācakā dhīre

saukhyārthini duḥkhārte sukhite kliṣṭe ca te pravartante|taddeśite ca dharme tattathatāyāṃ ca
dhīrāṇām

tasyāśca tathatārthatvāt kṣāntilābhādviśuddhitaḥ|karmadvayādanālambā maitrī kleśakṣayādapi

te niścalāśca calāśca kṛpaṇairāsvāditā na ca jñeyāḥ|acaleṣu bodhisattvāḥ pratiṣṭhitāḥ saktivigateṣu

asamāhitasvabhāvā mṛdumadhyā hīnabhūmikā ye'pi|hīnāśayāḥ samānā hīnāste hyanyathā tvadhikāḥ

brāhmyairvihṛtavihāraḥ kāmiṣu saṃjāyate yadā dhīmān|saṃbhārānpūrayate satvāṃśca vipācayati tena

sarvatra cāvirahito brāhmyai rahitaśca tadvipakṣeṇa|tatpratyayairapi bhṛśairna yāti vikṛtiṃ
pramatto 'pi

vyāpādavihiṃsābhyāmarativyāpādakāmarāgaiśca|yukto hi bodhisattvo bahuvidhamādīnavaṃ spṛśati

kleśairhantyātmānaṃ satvānupahanti śīlamupahanti|savilekhalābhahīno rakṣāhīnastathā śāstrā[tā]

sādhikaraṇo 'śayasvī paratra saṃjāyate 'kṣaṇeṣu sa ca|prāptāprāptavihīno manasi mahad duḥkhamāpnoti

ete sarve doṣā maitryādiṣu susthitasya na bhavanti|akliṣṭaḥ saṃsāraṃ satvārthaṃ no ca saṃtyajati

na tathaikaputrakeṣvapi guṇavatsvapi bhavati sarvasatvānāṃ|maitryādicetaneyaṃ satveṣu yathā
jinasutānāṃ

pradīptān śatruvaśagān duḥkhākrāntāṃstamovṛtān|durgamārgasamārūḍhānmahābandhanasaṃyutān

mahāśanaviṣākrāntalolānmārgapranaṣṭakān|utpathaprasthitān satvāndurbalān karuṇāyate

heṭhāpahaṃ hyuttamabodhibījaṃ sukhāvahaṃ tāya[pa]kamiṣṭahetuṃ|svabhāvadaṃ dharmamupāśritasya
bodhirna dūre jinātmajasya

vijñāya saṃsāragataṃ samagraṃ duḥkhātmakaṃ caiva nirātmakaṃ ca|nodvegamāyāti na cāpi doṣaiḥ
prabādhyate kāruṇiko 'grabuddhiḥ

duḥkhātmakaṃ lokamavekṣamāṇo duḥkhāyate vetti ca tadyathāvat|tasyābhyupāyaṃ parivarjane ca na
khedamāyatyapi vā kṛpāluḥ

kṛpā prakṛtyā pratisaṃkhyayā ca pūrvaṃ tadabhyāsavidhānayogāt|vipakṣahīnā ca viśuddhilābhāt
caturvidheyaṃ karūṇātmakānāṃ

na sā kṛpā yā na samā sadā vā nādhyāśayādvā pratipattito vā|vairāgyato nānupalambhato vā na
bodhisattvo hyakṛpastathā yaḥ

karuṇā kṣāntiścintā praṇidhānaṃ janmasatvaparipākaḥ|karuṇātarureṣa mahānmūlādiḥ puṣpapatra[paścimāgra]
(paścimānta)phalaḥ

mūlaṃ karuṇā na bhaved duṣkaracaryāsahiṣṇutā na bhavet|duḥkhākṣamaśca dhīmān satvārthaṃ cintayennaiva

cintāvihīnabuddhiḥ praṇidhānaṃ śuklajanmasu na kuryāt|śubhajanmānanugacchansatvānparipācayennaiva

karuṇāseko maitrī tadduḥkhe saukhyato vipulapuṣṭiḥ|śākhāvṛddhirviśadā yonimanaskārato jñeyā

parṇatyāgādānaṃ praṇidhīnāṃ saṃtateranucchedāt|dvividhapratyayasiddheḥ puṣpamabandhyaṃ phalaṃ
cāsmāt

kaḥ kurvīta na karuṇāṃ satveṣu mahākṛpāguṇakareṣu|duḥkhe'pi saukhyamatulaṃ bhavati yadeṣāṃ kṛpājanitaṃ

aviṣṭānāṃ kṛpayā na tiṣṭhati manaḥ śame kṛpālūnāṃ|kuta eva lokasaukhye svajīvite vā bhavetsnehaḥ

sneho na vidyate 'sau yo 'niravadyo na laukiko yaśca|dhīmatsu kṛpāsneho niravadyo lokasamatītaḥ

duḥkhājñānamahaughe mahāndhakāre ca niśritaṃ lokaṃ|uddhartuṃ ya upāyaḥ kathamiva na syātsa niravadyaḥ

sneho na so 'styarihatāṃ loke pratyekabodhibuddhānāṃ|prāgeva tadanyeṣāṃ kathamiva lokottaro
na syāt

duḥkhābhāve duḥkhaṃ yatkṛpayā bhavati bodhisattvānāṃ|saṃtrāsayati tadādau spṛṣṭaṃ tvabhinandayati
gāḍhaṃ

kimataḥ paramāścaryaṃ yad duḥkhaṃ saukhyamabhibhavati sarva|kṛpayā janitaṃ laukyaṃ yena vimukto
api kṛtārthaḥ

kṛpayā sahitaṃ dānaṃ yaddānasukhaṃ karoti dhīrāṇāṃ|traidhātukamupabhogairna tatsukhaṃ tatkalāṃ
spṛśati

duḥkhamayaṃ saṃsāraṃ yatkṛpayā na tyajati satvārthaṃ|parahitahetorduḥkhaṃ kiṃ kārūṇikairna samupetam

karūṇā dānaṃ bhogāḥ sadā kṛpālorvivṛddhimupayānti|snehānugrahajanitaṃ tacchaktikṛtaṃ sukhaṃ
cāsmāt

vardhe ca vardhayāmi ca dāne paripācayāmi sukhayāmi|ākarṣāmi nayāmi ca karuṇā sannānpravadatīva

duḥkhe duḥkhī kṛpayā sukhānyanādhāya kena sukhitaḥ syāt|sukhayatyātmānamataḥ kṛpālurādhāya parasaukhyam

svaṃ dānaṃ kārūṇikaḥ śāstīva sadaiva niḥsvasukhakāmaḥ|bhogaiḥ sukhaya paraṃ vā māmapyayutasaukhyam

saphalaṃ dānaṃ dattaṃ tanme satveṣu tatsukhasukhena|phala teṣveva nikāmaṃ yadi me kartavyatā
te 'sti

bhogedveṣṭurdāturbhogā bahuśubhataropasarpanti|na hi tatsukhaṃ mataṃ me dāne pāraṃparo 'smi
yataḥ

sarvāstiparityāge yatkṛpayā māṃ nirīkṣase satataṃ|nanu te tena jñeyaṃ na matphalenārthitā 'syeti

dānābhirato na syāṃ prāptaṃ cettatphalaṃ na visṛjeyaṃ|kṣaṇamapi dānena vinā dānābhirato bhavati
naiva

akṛtaṃ na phalasi yasmātpratikārāpekṣayā na me tulyaṃ|pratikāranirvyapekṣaḥ paratra phalado'sya
kāmaṃ te

niravadyaṃ śuddhapadaṃ hitāvahaṃ caiva sānurakṣaṃ ca|nirmṛgyaṃ nirlepaṃ jinātmajānāṃ kṛpādānam

sakalaṃ vipulaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ satataṃ muditaṃ nirāmiṣaṃ śuddhaṃ|bodhinataṃ kuśalanataṃ jinātmajānāṃ
kṛṣādānam

na tathopabhogatuṣṭiṃ labhate bhogī yathā parityāgāt|tuṣṭimupaiti kṛpāluḥ sukhatrayāpyāyitamanaskaḥ

kṛpaṇakṛpā raudrakṛpā saṃkṣubdhakṛpā kṛpā pramatteṣu|viṣayaparatantrakaruṇā mithyābhiniviṣṭakaruṇā
ca

karuṇā bodhisatvānāṃ sukhād duḥkhāttadanvayāt|karuṇā bodhisattvānāṃ hetormitrātsvabhāvataḥ

karuṇā bodhisattvānāṃ samā jñeyā tadāśayāt|pratipattervirāgācca nopalambhādviśuddhitaḥ

maitrādribhāvanāgrā svacittato dharmato 'dhimokṣācca|āśayato 'pi vibhutvādavikalpādaikyataścāpi

iti bhagavati jātasuprasādo mahadupadhidhruvasatkiryāmipūjī|bahuguṇahitamitranityasevo jagadanukampaka
eti sarvasiddhiṃ

|| mahāyānasūtrālaṃkāre pūjāsevā'pramāṇādhikāraḥ [ saptadaśaḥ] samāptaḥ ||
