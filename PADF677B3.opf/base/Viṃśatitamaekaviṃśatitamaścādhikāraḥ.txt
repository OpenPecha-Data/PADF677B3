viṃśatitamaekaviṃśatitamaścādhikāraḥliṅgavibhāge dvau ślokau |anukampā priyākhyānaṃ dhīratā
muktahastatā|gambhīrasaṃdhinirmokṣo liṅgānyetāni dhīmatāṃ

parigrahe 'dhimuktyāptāvakhede dvayasaṃgrahe|āśayācca prayogācca vijñeyaṃ liṅgapañcakaṃ

bodhisatvā hi satataṃ bhavantaścakravartinaḥ|prakurvanti hi satvārthaṃ gṛhiṇaḥ sarvajanmasu

ādānalabdhā pravrajyā dharmatopagatā parā|nidarśikā ca pravrajayā dhīmatāṃ sarvabhūmiṣu

aprameyairguṇairyuktaḥ pakṣaḥ pravrajitasya tu|gṛhiṇo bodhisatvāddhi yatistasmādviśiṣyate

paratreṣṭaphalecchā ca śubhavṛttāvihaiva ca|nirvāṇecchā ca dhīrāṇāṃ satveṣvāśaya iṣyate|aśuddhaśca
viśuddhaśca suviśuddhaḥ sarvabhūmiṣu

praṇidhānātsamāccittādādhipatyātparigrahaḥ|gaṇasya karṣaṇatvācca dhīmatāṃ sarvabhūmiṣu

karmaṇaścādhipatyena praṇidhānasya cāparā|samādheśca vibhutvasya cotpattirdhīmatāṃ matā

lakṣaṇātpudgagalācchikṣāskandhaniṣpattiliṅgataḥ|nirukteḥ prāptitaścaiva vihāro bhūmireva ca

śūnyatā paramātmasya karmānāśe vyavasthitiḥ|vihṛtya sasukhairdhyānairjanma kāme tataḥ param

tataśca bodhipakṣāṇāṃ saṃsāre pariṇāmanā|vinā ca cittasaṃkleśaṃ satvānāṃ paripācanā

upapattau ca saṃcitya saṃkleśasyānurakṣaṇā|ekāyanapathaśliṣṭā 'nimittaikāntikaḥ pathaḥ

animitte 'pyanābhogaḥ kṣetrasya ca viśodhanā|satvapākasya niṣpattirjāyate ca tataḥ param

samādhidhāraṇīnāṃ ca bodheścaiva viśuddhatā|etasmācca vyavasthānādvijñeyaṃ bhūmilakṣaṇam

viśuddhadṛṣṭiḥ suviśuddhaśīlaḥ samāhito dharmavibhūtamānaḥ|saṃtānasaṃkleśaviśuddhibhede nirmāṇa
ekakṣaṇalabdhabuddhiḥ

upekṣakaḥ kṣetraviśodhakaśca syātsatvapāke kuśalo maharddhiḥ|saṃpūrṇakāyaśca nidarśane ca śakto
'bhiṣiktaḥ khalu bodhisatvaḥ

dharmatāṃ pratividhyeha adhiśīle 'nuśikṣaṇe|adhicitte 'pyadhiprajñe prajñā tu dvayagocarā

dharmatatvaṃ tadajñānajñānādyā vṛttireva ca|prajñāyā gocarastasmād dvibhūmau tadvyavasthitiḥ

śikṣāṇāṃ bhāvanāyāśca phalamanyaccaturvidham|animittasasaṃskāro vihāraḥ prathamaṃ phalam

sa evānabhisaṃskāro dvitīyaṃ phalamiṣyate|kṣetraśuddhiśca satvānāṃ pākaniṣpattireva ca

samādhidhāraṇīnāṃ ca niṣpattiḥ paramaṃ phalaṃ|caturvidhaṃ phalaṃ hyetat caturbhūmisamāśritam

dharmatāṃ pratividhyeha śīlaskandhasya śodhanā|samādhiprajñāskandhasya tata ūrdhvaṃ viśodhanā

vimuktimuktijñānasya tadanyāsu viśodhanā|caturvidhādāvaraṇāt pratighātāvṛterapi

aniṣpannāśca niṣpannā vijñeyāḥ sarvabhūmayaḥ|niṣpannā apyaniṣpannā niṣpannāśca punarmatāḥ

niṣpattirvijñeyā yathāvyavasthānamanasikāreṇa|tatkalpanatājñānādavikalpanayā ca tasyaiva

bhāvanā api niṣpattiracintyaṃ sarvabhūmiṣu|pratyātmavedanīyatvāt buddhānāṃ viṣayādapi

adhimuktirhi sarvatra sālokā liṅgamiṣyate|alīnatvamadīnatvamaparapratyayātmatā

prativedhaśca sarvatra sarvatra samacittatā |aneyānunayopāyajñānaṃ maṇḍalajanma ca

nācchando na ca lubdhahrasvahṛdayo na krodhano nālasonāmaitrīkarūṇāśayo na kumatiḥ kalpairvikalpairhataḥ|no
vikṣiptamatiḥ sukhairna ca hato duḥkhairna vā [vyā]vartate|satyaṃ mitramupāśritaḥ
śrutaparaḥ pūjāparaḥ śāstari

sarvaṃ puṇyasamuccayaṃ suvipulaṃ kṛtvānyasādhāraṇaṃsaṃbodhau pariṇāmayatyaharaharyo hyuttamopāyavit|jātaḥ
svāyatane sadā śubhakaraḥ krīḍatyabhijñāguṇaiḥsarveṣāmuparisthito guṇanidhirjñeyaḥ
sa buddhātmanaḥ

śamathe vipaśyanāyāṃ ca dvayapañcātmako mataḥ|dhīmatāmanuśaṃso hi sarvathā sarvabhūmiṣu

paśyatāṃ bodhimāsannāṃ satvārthasya ca sādhanaṃ|tīvra utpadyate modo muditā tena kathyate

dauḥ śīlyābhogavaimalyādvimalā bhūmirucyate|mahādharmāvabhāsasya karaṇācca prabhākarī

arcirbhūtā yato dharmā bodhipakṣāḥ pradāhakāḥ|arciṣmatīti tadyogātsā bhūmirdvayadāhataḥ

satvānāṃ paripākaśca svacittasya ca rakṣaṇā|dhīmadbhirjīyate duḥkhaṃ durjayā tena kathyate

ābhimukhyād dvyasyeha saṃsārasyāpi nirvṛteḥ|uktā hyabhimukhī bhūmiḥ prajñāpāramitāśrayāt

ekāyanapathaśleṣādbhūmirdūraṃgamā matā|dvyasaṃjñāvicalanādacalā ca nirucyate

pratisaṃvinmatisādhutvādbhūmiḥ sādhumati matā|dharmameghā dvayavyāpterdharmākāśasya meghavat

vividhe śubhanirhāre ratyā viharaṇātsadā|sarvatra bodhisatvānāṃ vihārabhūmayo matāḥ

bhūyo bhūyo 'mitāsvāsu ūrdhvaṃgamanayogataḥ|bhūtāmitābhayārthāya ta eveṣṭā hi bhūmayaḥ

bhūmilābhe[bho]'dhimukteśca cariteṣu ca vartanāt|prativedhācca bhūmīnāṃ niṣpatteśca caturvidhaḥ

mahāyāne 'dhimuktānāṃ hīnayāne ca dehināṃ|dvayorāvarjanārthāya vinayāya ca deśitāḥ|caryāścatasro
dhīrāṇāṃ yathāsūtrānusārataḥ

anukampakasatveṣu saṃyogavigamāśaya|aviyogāśaya saukhyahitāśaya namo'stute

sarvāvaraṇanirmukta sarvalokābhibhū munejñānena jñeyaṃ vyāptaṃ te muktacitta namo'stute

aśeṣaṃ sarvasatvānāṃ sarvakleśavināśaka|kleśaprahāraka kliṣṭasānukrośa namo'stute

anābhoga nirāsaṅga avyāghāta samāhita|sadaiva sarvapraśnānāṃ visarjaka namo'stu te

āśraye 'thāśrite deśye vākye jñāne ca deśike|avyāhatamate nityaṃ sudeśika namo'stute

upetya vacanaisteṣāṃ carijña āgatau gatau|niḥ sāre caiva satvānāṃ svavavāda namo'stu te

satpauruṣyaṃ prapadyante tvāṃ dṛṣṭvā sarvadehinaḥ|dṛṣṭamātrātprasādasya vidhāyaka namo 'stu
te

ādānasthānasaṃtyāganirmāṇapariṇāmane|samādhijñānavaśitāmanuprāpta namo'stu te

upāye śaraṇe śuddhau satvānāṃ vipravādane|mahāyāne ca niryāṇe mārabhañja namo 'stu te

jñānaprahāṇaniryāṇavighnakārakadeśika|svaparārthe 'nyatīrthyānāṃ nirādhṛṣya namo 'stu te

vi[ni]gṛhyavaktā parṣatsu dvyasaṃkleśavarjita|nirārakṣa asaṃmoṣa gaṇakarṣa namo'stu te

cāre vihāre sarvatra nāstyasarvajñaceṣṭitaṃ|sarvadā tava sarvajña bhūtārthika namo'stu te

sarvasattvārthakṛtyeṣu kālaṃ tvaṃ nātivartase|abandhyakṛtya satatamasaṃmoṣaḥ namo'stu te

sarvalokamahorātraṃ ṣaṭkṛtvaḥ pratyavekṣase|mahākaruṇayā yukta hitāśaya namo'stu te

cāreṇādhigamenāpi jñānenāpi ca karmaṇā|sarvaśrāvakapratyekabuddhottama namo'stu te

tribhiḥ kāyairmahābodhiṃ sarvākārāmupāgata|sarvatra sarvasatvānāṃ kāṅkṣāchida namo'stu te

niravagraha nirdoṣa niṣkāluṣyānavasthita|āniṅkṣya sarvadharmeṣu niṣprapañca namo'stu te

niṣpannaparamārthoṃ 'si sarvabhūmiviniḥsṛtaḥ|sarvasatvāgratāṃ prāptaḥ sarvasatvavimocakaḥ

akṣayairasamairyukto guṇairlokeṣu dṛśyase|maṇḍaleṣvapyadṛśyaśca sarvathā devamānuṣaiḥ

'dhikāraḥ |||| samāptaśca mahāyānasūtrālaṃkāra iti ||
